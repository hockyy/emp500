Tips:

  • k-row: add dakuten ゛ to convert to g-row. Example: か ka→ が ga
  • s-row: add dakuten ゛ to convert to z-row. Example: さ sa→ ざ za

  • h-row: add dakuten ゛ to convert to b-row. Example: は ha→ ば ba
